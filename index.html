What is Nashville Hot Chicken?
Nashville Hot Chicken is a style of fried chicken originated in Nashville, Tennessee. Chicken is fried, then dipped a spicy oil of your choosing, and dusted with a spice blend for a flavorful, and SPICY fried chicken experience
What is a smashburger?
Smashburgers are different from other burgers in that they are literally smashed from a regular patty size onto a scorching hot grill resulting in a delicious crust with thin well done patties packed with flavor.

-Parking garage on Church Street or Paterson Street
